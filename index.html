PROJECT DESCRIPTION

4-TECH TEAM

Overview:

Our project is about creating a website that would become a useful tool for newcomers /beginners to the IT world. In this website, we will have these main features: Basic guidelines to Python, HTML, JAVA, C++, etc. (this part we would also have videos to demonstrate the codeline), a sharing page from other advanced coders on tips and on where to start in programming, a place where our aimed users can get a certificate from us in achieving their course.

Motivation:

Our motivation for this project comes from the personal experiences of ourselves when we first started in programming. We found that we were lost on our way to the purpose in this IT world and we were a bit struggling to understand the concepts/codesline. So we hope that this project can be a place to help users in defining their ways in the industry and could somehow approach the lessons easier than we did in the past. And from our side of this, we get to learn more about working as a team, get to share our knowledge and combine them together as we all have different parts of required knowledge used to make the website.

Description:

The introduction of our team Project would start with the deep meaning of our Project: 4tech. It is a combination of four elements: Academic, Sharing, New, Credit, and Qualifications. 4tech strives to advance into a contribution that users entrance to hit the books and a diverse channel of instruction supporting you in your performance and maps.

a. Academic:

The academic part will be to play an essential role in explaining the most. At this platform, students will have more opportunity to experience, and education such as training through many lectures and the practices will be uploaded and renewed regularly of 4-Tech. Following the conversations is a comprehensive video tutorial. In the lessons, learners also can cultivate a specialized technical English vocabulary via in the learning process.

- Lectures: Many programming languages including Python, Java, C, C ++, C # will be integrated into the lecturers.

- Exercise: Students can test their own knowledge by completing application exercises after each new lesson.

- Videos: Today, with the advent of many academic websites for programming with simple documentation tutorials but rarely detailed video tutorials. It is difficult for beginners to understand and overwhelmed when visiting these websites. Therefore, the combination of lectures with tutorial videos will be one of the useful solutions.

- Vocabulary: The noted vocabulary is usually technical words; students will encounter these words in many documents or other technical articles.

b. Sharing:

Articles sharing knowledge and learning experiences, which are gathered from people who have been in the industry before, will be posted and archived in a separate directory. Some examples include: Sharing work experience at Google, How to conquer Python, Experience in applying for a technical job and The opportunity of Data Science in the future.

Besides, the career path is an indispensable part of the sharing page. In technology industry, each difference direction of working will accompany the different learning and practice pathway. 4-Tech will summarise and recommend in detail about a learning path suitable for the purpose that supports students can undoubtedly determine their way of studies and careers in the future.

c. News:

Category news will be the highlight of the website. This feature will be continuously updated with information related to the field of technology, including AI projects, Robots, which technologies are being researched, and how the revolutions that technology results in life. This feature also allows students to refer to recruitment information for many different career opportunities and job positions in technology with job requirements and descriptions.

Updating a lot of news and our current knowledge would be necessary as well as identifying the requirements of employers now; our insights, which are a vital division that brings success on the future career path, will become competent yourself..

d. Credits or Qualifications:

4-Tech will promote educational programs by partnering with a number of colleges such as RMIT University, Harvard, MIT and major technology centers to provide some valuable degrees and loans when students pass the final competency test and complete the course.

Students will have to take the final exam relevant with the knowledge learned by many types of questions or undertake their own science and engineering projects to achieve a 4-Tech certificate, which is the reinforcement of quality of the curriculum vitae (CV) in a job interview or applying for a higher level scholarship, after completing the course.

Problem solved

Information technology is rapidly changing that both neophytes and professionals must keep learning and practising in order not to be eliminated and outdated with advanced technology. However, beginners may find it struggling to choose whether software, programming languages, skills are suitable for them and later in their career options. This website can help newcomers have an overview type of IT major, from required skills to the prospect of demandable jobs in the future and teach you how to obtain necessary skills. For instance, what a full-stack engineer would do, what is data science, how different between a software architect and a solution architect, etc.
Also, our website can help users learn by online courses, which they can save time of traveling and be able to review their course again whenever they finish learning. Another advantage is during the Covid-19, streaming online courses prevent the danger of studying face to face in classes by learning in a far distance, which is applied in many countries.

Skill required:

- HTML/CSS programming language skills
- UI (User Interface), UX (User Experience) designing skills by Adobe Creative Cloud
- Working with Pycharm, Git and GitHub (GitHub repository)
- Debugging and solving problems skills
- Communication and connectivity capabilities
- Understanding the demand of IT in educational industries

Outcome:

The target of this project is to orient introductory students in the information technology field to learn the basics of programming, including two main languages, which are Python and HTML. Our team hopes to finish the project by creating a temporary website platform that is closest to complete with user-friendly and easy-to-use features. In addition, with all four members of the 4-TECH group being students of information technology, we understand the difficulty of approaching new ideas for the first time. 4-TECH brings this project with the desire to create a learning forum, sharing knowledge from seniors with the aim of promoting a passion for a programming subject. In the first phase of the project, our team will achieve a demo with the least amount of bugs within its limits and bring a positive effect and also a meaningful gift from 4-Tech for new students when the project is completed.
